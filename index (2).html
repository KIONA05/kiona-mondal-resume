About

A STEM mind drawn to high finance

I'm a business and finance enthusiast with a non-traditional journey—from a STEM background into the world of startups, consulting, and high finance.

What started as curiosity quickly turned into intent. Through experiences like participating in the Hult Prize and engaging in competitive academic environments, I've developed a strong interest in how businesses are built, scaled, and valued.

Currently, I'm focused on building a strong foundation in finance, business strategy, and analytical thinking, while actively seeking opportunities that challenge me and accelerate my learning.

Long term, I aim to work at the intersection of finance and entrepreneurship—contributing to high-impact decisions and eventually building ventures of my own.
