Our Story

About Aura

Born from a passion for beauty and a belief that everyone deserves to feel extraordinary.

12+Years of Excellence
Meet the Founder

Priya Sinha

Founder & Creative Director

Priya Sinha's journey into beauty started at the age of seventeen, watching her mother apply kajal in the warm morning light of their home in South Kolkata. That simple ritual of care planted a seed that grew into Aura Wellness Salon.

After graduating from the prestigious VLCC Institute of Beauty & Nutrition and training under master stylists in Mumbai and London, Priya returned to Kolkata with one mission: to create a sanctuary where every woman — and man — walks out feeling genuinely transformed.

In 2013, she opened the first Aura studio with just four chairs and a vision. Today, Aura is Kolkata's most beloved full-service wellness salon, with a team of 28 trained professionals and thousands of loyal clients.
